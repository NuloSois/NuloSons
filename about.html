Hello, my name is Davi, I'm a brazilian Musician, Developer and a lot of other things unrelated to this site. I've been playing Guitar and Composing since January 2020, having started taking formal lessons with more rigor
since April 2022, along with Keyboard classes. My main musical interests are: Rock/Metal (and their subgenres),
Electronic, Classical (mainly from the romantic and modern periods and MPB/Bossa Nova, among other genres.
Music is one of the passions pf my life and one of the thing I live for, so I may get too passionate about the topic from
time to time.
I have released two LPs since 2020, one of which I'm not proud to share, the other, differently, Is Nu Mun, which
is indexed in the site. I also have lots of still unreleased music, be it in production or finished, ranging from
Classical Compositions to Guitar Demos.

Now a List of gear I use for my music:

  • Guitar: Modified Tagima Guitar (Probably a Stella or a TG-510).
  • Amp: Frahm CA-909.
  • DAW: FL Studio 12.
  • Mic: Unprofessional.
  • ScoreWriter: Musescore 3.
  • Video Editor: VEGAS Pro 18.

    • As much I enjoy making music, I also very much enjoy listening and analyzing the music of others, for that reason
    I also intend to have a Section for Reviews, Playlists and Recomendations in this site. This is improtant not only
    for the sake of sharing great music but also for drawing the map of influences and interests of me as a musician.
